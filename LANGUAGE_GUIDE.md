TONE GUIDELINES FOR ALL APP COPY

DO Write Like This:
Casual: "jot down thoughts"
Helpful: "the more you log, the more accurate"
Honest: "not possible at your pace"
Encouraging: "nice work staying ahead"
Specific: "need 47 pages/day with 8 days left"
Action-oriented: "log pages" not "track progress"

DON'T Write Like This:
Clinical: "calculate reading velocity"
Vague: "improve your efficiency"
Judgmental: "you're falling behind"
Robotic: "please input data"
Corporate: "optimize your workflow"
Complicated: "prioritize based on temporal proximity”
